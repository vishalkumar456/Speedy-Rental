About Us

Speedy Rentals is the best emerging car renting provider in Pakistan awarded at the 15th Consumer Choice Awards.
Speedy Rentals cars in the past was nothing less of a hassle. Traditional renting industry called for several rounds finding a car of your choice, inconsistent rates, and low quality assurance. Speedy Rental is the first on-demand car rental service that solves all these problems on a single platform.

What we do

Speedy Rental’s car-rental services are available at your fingertips online, for a more modern and convenient customer experience. Get the car you want, at the time you asked for.

Speedy Rental provides both on-demand and pre-scheduled vehicles for any situation, occasion or event, such as:

  • Corporate Office Staff
  • Tours and Trips
  • Weddings
  • Families
  • Individuals
One of the biggest strengths of Speedy Rentals is its versatility. Our wide-range of latest models and well-maintained cars, is suited for everyone and all occasions.

With Speedy Rentals, you get what you want. We rent cars with a chauffeur as well as on a self drive basis.

Our mission
Car rental simplified 

Speedy Rentals was established with our primary focus on you, our customers. It all began with the notion of evolving the car-rental industry into a customer-centric and advanced solution with a fresh, honest approach.

When booking a hire vehicle, Speedy Rental aims to provide hand picked vehicles, trustworthy drivers, all at the convenience of your home.

At Speedy Rental, each car and driver must undergo ‘Speedy Rental Selection Tests’ before becoming operational. Quality, being the cornerstone of Speedy Rental services, is ensured in every mile.

With your safety and comfort in mind, Speedy Rental's makes car rental simplified for everyone.
